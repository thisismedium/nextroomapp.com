Why NextRoom App?

  • You can begin using NextRoom almost immediately.

    Once your account is activated and set up, NextRoom requires minimal staff training to use.

  • Manage your patient queue.

    Instantly see how many patients are waiting to be seen and in what order.

  • Reclaim your freedom.

    Instant alerts when a patient is added to your queue.

  • Transfers rooms between staff.

    NextRoom helps you quickly add rooms to a physician and a nurse queue with notes and procedures.

  • Quickly find other doctors and nurses, anywhere.

    The occupied screen will easily show the location of staff when checked into a room.

  • Half-mast the flag system and embrace efficiency.

    The medical flag system is outdated. We've taken the flexibility of the flags and created a service that actually helps you run your practice.

  • Nothing is left to interpretation.

    Trade real information and notes without having to memorize flag color combinations.

  • One plan, one price.

    Once your NextRoom service is activated, add as many users to your account as needed with no additional cost.
