Why NextRoom App?

  • It's not open for interpretation.

    Lose the confusing "flag code" and trade real information between staff.

  • Manage your patient queue.

    See how many patients are waiting to be seen on one screen and in the proper order.

  • Reclaim your freedom.

    Be alerted when a new patient is added to your queue from anywhere - all from your iPhone or iPod Touch.

  • Assign exam rooms to staff.

    EMR schedulers may get a patient there at a certain time, but NextRoom makes certain that the patient sees the proper physician in the proper order.

  • Stop wasting time looking for staff.

    When a nurse or physician accepts a room, the system shows all users your current location.

  • Move on from the flag system and get efficient.

    The flag system has run its course. Now you can know where all your patients are and in what order they're to be seen.

  • One plan, one price.

    Once you activate your NextRoom service, add as many users to your account as needed.
